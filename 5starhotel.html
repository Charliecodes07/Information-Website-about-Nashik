Top 5 Star Hotels in Nashik

In today's chaotic urban lifestyle, when everyone is craving relaxation, there is no better place to be in than the luxurious hotels in Nashik. And what's there not to like about a day full of entertainment, relaxation and joy.

Radisson Blu Hotel & Spa, Nashik

Hotel Overview : Nestled at the foothills of the two-millennia old Pandav Caves, discover an idyllic setting for a luxury getaway at the Radisson Blu Hotel & Spa, Nashik. This modern 5-star urban hotel, positioned as the flagship property of the group, boasts unparalleled views of the mountains and untouched scenery fringed by abundant lush green landscape.
Vikrikar Bhavan Road, Pathardi Phata,Nashik

Viveda Wellness Resort

Hotel Overview: Situated in Nashik, 8.8 km from Someshwar Temple, Viveda Wellness Resort features accommodation with free bikes, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool and a garden. This 5-star resort offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The accommodation offers evening entertainment and luggage storage space
Beze Village, Trimbak Road, Nashik,

Courtyard by Marriott Nashik

Hotel Overview: Located in Nashik, 2.6 km from Shri Kalaram Sansthan Mandir, Courtyard by Marriott Nashik provides accommodation with free bikes, free private parking, a fitness centre and a terrace. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. The accommodation offers a concierge service, a tour desk and currency exchange for guests.
Mumbai Naka, Mumbai-Agra National Highway, Nashik

Aria Resort & Spa

Hotel Overview: Aria Resort & Spa welcomes you to Nashik City. Set among lush greens, It will transform your get-away into an encounter of the faculties and emotions. Located near Gangapur dam with 180 degree of water view, as it streams gloriously by the peaceful village encompassed by the sublime Sahyadri Ranges. The Premises is only 5 min away from #sulavineyards and equipped with all the accommodations to ensure your comfort and relaxation. Aria Resort and Spa offers Luxurious accommodation with a infinity pool having an outrageous view along with restaurant and bar.
Gangaware-Savargaon Road, Gangaware, Nashik

The Gateway Hotel Ambad

Hotel Overview: The Gateway Hotel Ambad Nashik is conveniently located within touching distance to prominent tourist attractions in and around Nashik. At 160 kms, our hotel in Nashik is also the perfect destination for a blissful getaway from Mumbai. Enjoy our warm hospitality and service that make you feel at home. Explore the all meals inclusive 4D Travel offer to plan your next stay.
MIDC Ambad, Mumbai - Agra Road Nashik

Express Inn The Business Luxury Hotel

Hotel Overview:If you looking to spoil yourselves in the lap of nature accompanied with the best of wines, then nashik and the express inn hotel is the place for you. Definitely, not over-hyped, clean and top-class service, the express inn hotel is the first hotel that greets you as you enter the city. It's not overly priced and you can be rest assured that it's completely worth your money. Sula vineyards is about 30 mins from this place while renaissance wines, another excellent vinery, is about 35 mins from the express inn. All in all, it's was a great weekend stay with very cool and calm weather to spoil yourself.
MIDC Ambad Nashik
