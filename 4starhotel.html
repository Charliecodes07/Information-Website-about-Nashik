Top 4 Star Hotels in Mumbai

In today's chaotic urban lifestyle, when everyone is craving relaxation, there is no better place to be in than the luxurious hotels in Mumbai. And what's there not to like about a day full of entertainment, relaxation and joy.

The Source at Sula

Hotel Overview : The overall experience is fantastic. Right from check in there is dedicated person to help you with all your request. The rooms are european style with minimum furniture & well maintained. There are multiple food options with 3 to 4 different restaurants. You can enjoy a swim at the pool & also jacuzzi is available next to the pool. Enjoy the nature around, cycle if u are interested. Take a tour of the vine factory followed by vine tasting. Enjoy live music & barbeque at the pool side in evening.
Gangavhare ,Nashik

Grape County Eco Resort & Spa, Nashik

Hotel Overview:Set in 49 hectares of land and next to protected forest, this relaxed ecolodge across from NH848 national highway is 10 km from the Brahmagiri mountains and 3 km from the Coin Museum. Low-key rooms and permanent tents offer air-conditioning, flat-screens and safes, along with minibars, tea and coffeemaking facilities, and balconies or patios. Upgraded rooms with wood-panelled ceilings add lounge areas. Hilltop 2-bedroom, glass-enclosed villas feature terraces and forest views. Amenities include a casual restaurant featuring bike-themed decor and an outdoor terrace. There's also an amphitheatre and a nature park. Outdoor activities are offered.
Anjaneri, Trimbakeshwar Road Nashik

Suma Adventure and Resort

Hotel Overview:For all the Adventure Junkies out there, SUMA Adventure and Resort is a new destination where you can spend an adventurous and trilling day. An adventure park where you can enjoy a fun filled day with your family and friends. Located amidst majestic mountains and serene lakeside in Nasik, SUMA Adventure & Resort is a perfect getaway to visit from Mumbai and Pune. It offers multiple exciting adventure activities to give individuals as well as groups, a thrilling experience.
Trambakeshwar Rd, Mahiravni Nashik

Swiss Traffalgaar Luxury Hotels

Hotel Overview:Even work will feel like a vacation at the Swiss Traffalgaar Luxury Hotels. The spa, fitness centre and a picnic area are bound to make your stay memorable. Sweat your stress away at the in-house fitness centre.Have a fun-filled time at the hotel's picnic area. For your sweet tooth, the hotel also has its own cafe that is open for 24 hours.Pamper yourself with a relaxing therapy at the spa present at the property.
MIDC Ambad, Nashik

Beyond by Sula | Rooms & Pool

Hotel Overview:Sula vineyards has multiple stay options in nashik near the gangapur dam. We choose to stay at the beyond. It has pres-tine views of the dam backwaters and a walk way which goes near shore. The property is extremely well maintained with excellent service as compared to other resorts in the area. The price which you pay to stay here is worth it! normally big resorts have a little delayed service however here we got instant room service. Earloer we had planned to go outside for dinner however the food was the highlight and we had multiple meals here itself. We got our tastebuds filled with indian as well as continental food
Gangavhare, Nashik

Kyriad Hotel Nashik by OTHPL

Hotel Overview:Set along the busy AH47 road, this informal hotel is 3 km from Pandav Leni Caves and 8 km from Shri Kalaram Sansthan Mandir Vedic Puja Spiritual Centre.The relaxed rooms and suites have Wi-Fi, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking equipment. Some include sitting areas. Amenities consist of an international restaurant and a coffee shop, in addition to a gym. A breakfast buffet is available.
Rane Nagar, Mumbai-Agra National Hwy
