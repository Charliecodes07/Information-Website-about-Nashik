Nashik - Local Market

Saraf Bazaar

Detail: Saraf Bazaar is one of the best places for shopping in Nashik. Beautiful rings, bangles, necklaces, anklets and so much more can be found here. This is one of the busiest bazaars in the city and you can see women of all ages fawning over exquisite jewellery here. Find your treasure and get a gift for someone too. Get bedazzled by the sparkle of these trinkets

What to buy:Beautiful rings, bangles, necklaces, anklets and so much more

Opening hour: Daily from morning until night.

Location: Panchavati, Nashik

Deolali Camp Market

Detail: Due to its fame for temples and army base, many tourists visit Deolali camp. There are many shops from which you can buy antiques, artworks and mementoes. There are many restaurants and street food shops where you can enjoy the local delicacies. Visit Deolali camp for a unique experience.

What to buy:Antiques, bronze items, vintage items,

Opening hour: Daily from morning until night.

Location: Deolali Camp ,Nashik

Tibetan Market

Detail: Famous for winter ware like jackets. woollen gloves, beanies and many more, the Tibetan market is one of the famous street markets in Nashik. Along with woollen clothes, Tibetan market offers a wide range of western clothes. Men's clothing and accessories can be found at cheap rates. Hungry after shopping? There are many street food stalls selling mouth-watering local delicacies. Visit the Tibetan market in Nashik for a complete shopping experience.

What to buy: jackets. woollen gloves, beanies and many more.

Opening hour: Daily from morning until night.

Location: Sharanpur Road, Anandwan Colony,
