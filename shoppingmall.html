Shopping - Mall

While the local and traditional markets have held their own till day, Nashik has also, over the years, built a reputation for being one of the most modern Indian cities, a hub of fashion and style and the launching pad of everything international and global. It is cities to bring in the concept of shopping malls and living up to this image, it has some of the best ones in the india.

City Centre mall

Detail: City Centre mall in Nashik is the biggest shopping mall in the city. CCM is established by the Sarda Group along with its partners "Yatra Capital". The Nashik City Centre is a great mall with a good layout that gives a sense of space and flow. There are plenty of shopping options from budget-friendly to premium brands. Easy access and parking. The food court has a good variety to choose from
Highlight: Luxury Fashion Brands
10:00 AM to 10:00 PM
Untwadi Rd ,Nashik

Pinnacle Mall

Detail: Pinnacle mall is one of the top malls in Nasik. Variety of shops to visit for. Haircut, gaming section, Brand factory and Grocery Store, Reliance Smart Store, Jewelery and Perfumes. Mall is located at very central location of the city. Lot's of small fast food vendors are also present here. Do visit for casual shopping and fun centre to have fun!! Easy to locate in the centre of the city
Highlight:Reliance Smart Store , gaming
11:00 AM to 10:00 PM
Shopping Mall in Mohan Nagar
